╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                         SAFETY NOTES                                         ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

  ⚠️  ESP32 operates at 3.3V logic - DO NOT connect to 5V sources!
  ⚠️  Always use current-limiting resistor with LED (220-330Ω)
  ⚠️  Check LED polarity: Long leg = Anode (+), Short leg = Cathode (-)
  ⚠️  Maximum current per GPIO pin: 12mA (40mA absolute maximum)
  ⚠️  Total current for all GPIO pins: 200mA maximum
